About Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, made up of over 1,000 coral islands grouped into 26 atolls. It is famous for its crystal-clear turquoise lagoons, colourful coral reefs, and overwater bungalows. Offering some of the finest snorkelling and diving in the world, the Maldives is the ultimate escape for those seeking serenity and natural beauty.
